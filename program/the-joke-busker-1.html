The Joke Busker

National Young Writers Festival

Chris Knight wanted to be a paid comedy writer, so taking a cue from poetry buskers, he hit the streets of Adelaide.

What is Joke Busking? Artisanal, hand-crafted jokes made to order based on a word of your own suggestion? Or some guy doing typewritten puns on the street? Both.
